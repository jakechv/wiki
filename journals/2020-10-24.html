01:56 NATs

src What is the future of the internet? The need for global collectivity is super important; one of the biggest blockers is NAT traversal. NAT :: Network Address Translation. A mechanism used to map from one IP address space to another to redirect IP packets in transit. We must prevent every connected device from requiring a public IP, so they should be freely redirected to our own private subnetworks from our router to the local devices connected to it.
